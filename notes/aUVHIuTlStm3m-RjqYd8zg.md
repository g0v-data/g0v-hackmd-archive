# Case study 

### City of Vincent (Norfolk Street) Norfolk St & Burt St 

![](https://g0v.hackmd.io/_uploads/HylHBkFsZxg.jpg)

![](https://g0v.hackmd.io/_uploads/HyggwMYi-ll.png)


In the photo, roadblocks can be seen in the distance, indicating that what was originally a through road has been converted into a cul-de-sac. Along this route, there is a senior living complex, and at the end of the road, there is a large local park. Passing through the park leads toward the riverside area near the city center.

片中道路遠處可以看到設置車阻，原本應該是直通道路，改為囊底路；此路徑沿線有一處高齡生活園區，以及道路末端有一個地區大型公園，穿過公園就接近市中心的河岸水域
