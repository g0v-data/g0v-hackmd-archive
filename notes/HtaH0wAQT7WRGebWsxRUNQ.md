What Exactly is a Kundali and Why Does It Matter?
A Kundali is essentially a snapshot of the sky at the precise time and place you were born. It maps out the positions of the sun, moon, and planets across twelve different houses, each representing a unique area of your life like career, health, and romance. Understanding your birth chart is not about predicting a rigid, unchangeable future. Instead, it is about self-discovery and gaining a deeper understanding of your natural strengths, challenges, and hidden potentials.
