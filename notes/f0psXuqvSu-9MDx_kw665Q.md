**The membranes market, based on material types, is segmented into ceramic, polymeric and others.**

Among them, the polymeric segment, due to its versatility and cost-effectiveness, dominated the market in 2023. Generally speaking, polymeric membranes, like PVDF, polysulfone, and polyamide, are usually prepared and applied to major industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing due to the fact that these kinds of materials are rather easy to make and flexible, resistant to chemicals.

While industries continue to find ways of cutting costs while sustaining high filtration efficiency, polymeric membranes remain among the preferred options in the market.
