## Eric Gordon

[https://elab.emerson.edu/people/eric-gordon](https://elab.emerson.edu/people/eric-gordon)

Eric Gordon 是 Engagement Lab 的創辦人，美國 Emerson College 視覺媒體藝術系的教授，同時也是哈佛 Berkman Center for Internet and Society 的一員，研究數位公民參與、遊戲化學習。著有 [Net Locality: Why Location Matters in a Networked World](http://www.amazon.com/Net-Locality-Location-Matters-Networked/dp/1405180609)、[Civic Media: Technology|Design|Practice](https://mitpress.mit.edu/civic-media) 。

Engagement Lab
[https://elab.emerson.edu/](https://elab.emerson.edu/)

### Projects:

[PARTICIPATORY POKÉMON GO](https://elab.emerson.edu/projects/participatory-pokemon-go) is video competition to get youth to think about the relationship between civic data and community values using a popular augmented reality game.

[Y-ADAPT](https://elab.emerson.edu/projects/y-adapt) Y-Adapt is a curriculum consisting of games and playful activities that helps youth to understand climate change and take climate adaptation actions in their community.

[AR STORIES HACK DAY](https://elab.emerson.edu/projects/ar-stories-hack-day) A hack-a-thon for Boston youth to commemorate Boston’s local history in augmented reality games by Niantic.

[A COMMUNITY APPROACH TO RESEARCH PARTNERSHIPS (CARP)](https://elab.emerson.edu/projects/a-community-approach-to-research-partnerships-carp) is an effort to develop models for effective community-academic partnerships

[@STAKE](https://elab.emerson.edu/projects/atstake) is a role-playing game that fosters democracy, empathy, and creative problem solving for civic issues.

[HYGIENE WITH CHHOTA BHEEM](https://elab.emerson.edu/projects/hygiene-with-chhota-bheem) Hygiene with Chhota Bheem is a game-based curriculum designed to encourage toilet use and handwashing with soap among 8-12 year old children in India.
