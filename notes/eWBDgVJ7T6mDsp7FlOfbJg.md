 - Caleb: 
     - A new ID is a decent idea, but it doesn't necessarily solve the problem of Taiwan being fully pwned. You can still just look someone up by their name and then filter based on other attributes you already know about them. Also, a new ID doesn't solve the actual problem, or prevent futher leaks in the future.
     - 一個新的身份證號碼是個不錯的主意，但這並不一定能解決台灣完全被攻陷的問題。你仍然可以只憑他們的名字查找某人，然後根據你已經知道的其他特徵進行篩選。此外，一個新的身份證號碼無法解決實際問題，也無法防止未來的洩露。
     - The problem is that Taiwan government organizations and businesses are very vulnerable to being pwned. There's no centralized policy for data protection, and there's no centralized knowledge base or skillset on methods for securing citizen data for the various government agencies. If you change everyone's ID, they'll probably just get pwned again, and all your effort will have been basically for nothing (for that solution).
     - 問題在於台灣政府組織和企業很容易受到攻擊。沒有集中的資料保護政策，也沒有關於保護公民資料的集中知識庫或技能組。如果你更改每個人的身份證號碼，他們可能會再次被攻陷，你的努力將基本上一無所獲（對於該解決方案）。
     - The treatment for the root disease must involve securing everyone's data better, and protecting against digital attack. 
     - 根本病因的治療必須涉及更好地保護每個人的資料，並防止數位攻擊。
     - A good strategy to ensure this would be to choose a government agency or ministry that's in charge of directing data and digital security for all agencies, ministries, and businesses. This entity would be a "single source of truth." 
     - 確保此目標的一個好策略是選擇一個負責指導所有機構、部門和企業的資料和數位安全的政府機構或部門。這個實體將成為“唯一真實來源”。
     - Because transparency and open-source values in the digital space lead to more secure results, such an entity should establish processes and techniques in an open-source and transparent way. This way citizens can see for themselves that good, secure, techniques are being used. This also reduces the cost overhead of centralizing national digital security, because private citizens can contribute to auditing the entity's policies and techniques.
     - 由於數位空間中的透明度和開源價值觀會導致更安全的結果，因此這樣的實體應以開源和透明的方式建立流程和技術。這樣，公民可以親自查看正在使用的良好、安全的技術。這還降低了集中國家數位安全成本，因為私人公民可以為審查該實體的政策和技術做出貢獻。
     - The somewhat new MODA in Taiwan is perfectly positioned to function in this role as "single source of truth" on securing data. As a newer agency, it may still be "finding its place" within the Taiwanese bureaucracy. It's possible that other ministries are hesitant to give over any authority over their digital systems to MODA because of politics. 
     - 台灣相對新的MODA非常適合擔任保護資料的“唯一真實來源”角色。作為一個較新的機構，它可能仍在台灣官僚體系中“尋找自己的位置”。其他部門可能會因為政治原因而不願將其數位系統的任何權力移交給MODA。
     - The recent data breach represents a good political opportunity for MODA to be given further authority over managing digital security in Taiwan (public and private). 
     - 最近的資料外洩事件為MODA提供了一個很好的政治機會，可以進一步掌管台灣（公共和私人部門）的數位安全。
     - To further our goal of treating the problem at the source, we should communicate with MODA and collaborate on techniques for leveraging this opportunity. The suggested ideas of generating memes and other content is good. These strategies can be used to convince various citizens to push pressure on their local government officials to work with MODA on securing citizen data. 
     - 為了從根本上解決問題，我們應該與MODA溝通並合作，利用這次機會制定策略。生成模因和其他內容的建議想法很好。這些策略可以用來說服各種公民向當地政府官員施加壓力，與MODA合作保護公民資料。
