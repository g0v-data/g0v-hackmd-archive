大選又要到了， 今年投票該怎麼投呢？ 你真的認識這些候選人嗎？

投票指南裡，「 找縣市長」、「 找議員」 清楚整理了縣市長和議員的學經歷、 議案、 表決、 政治獻金、 財產申報以及工程建議款， 讓你對候選人的背景和公職生涯的實際作為一目了然； 

針對初次參選的候選人， 可以在「給你好看-我是候選人」 上發布自己的資料和短片， 讓大家更了解自己的背景和理念。

「 找提案」 依各區列出議員和政府在議會的提案，你可以和大家一起對各提案發表意見；

「給你好看-公投」 也包含2018年10案公投的領銜人、主文、 遞交中選會理由書和詳細提案歷程。

Here comes the big 9-in-one election. Which candidate should you cast a vote? Do you really know these candidates?

In Vote-Taiwan, "Find-Major", "Find-Councilor" clearly arrange informations including education, work experience, motions and vote in council, political contribution, property declearation and project proposal, making it easy to realize background and result of every candidate.

For first-joined candidates, "See More - Candidates" gives a plateform to publish information even a short film to introduc themself.

"Bills" shows prosals of councilor or local government in each constituency. You can comments to proposals with others.

"See More" provides 10 referendoms in 2018. Here you can see the proposer, content, summited proposal form and detail course of each proposal.
