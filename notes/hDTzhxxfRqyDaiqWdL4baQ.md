## What we can do with these...

So aside from things we mentioned we could do:

- Automated evaluation of the quality of information.
- Traceability: 
  - the patient zero
  - distribution of information from source to people’s chat group. 
- Study the quality/kind of information that regular Taiwanese people are receiving on a daily basis and/or how it’s interacted with major events in the country. 
- Study the use of words in news/articles titles.
- Compare the point of views of various news websites or content farms.
- Facebook friendship timeline -- when did your mom drift away from you in terms of political opinions?
- … More proposals WELCOMEEEEE
