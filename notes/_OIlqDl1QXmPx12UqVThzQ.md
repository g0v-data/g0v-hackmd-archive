Rugby is a dynamic sport that blends physical strength with strategic teamwork. It involves players advancing a ball to score points, using both their hands and feet, while adhering to specific rules that test their coordination and resilience. For those interested in exploring rugby or netball further, [Centaurs](https://www.centaursgroup.com/) offers comprehensive training programs and community groups. These sports are perfect avenues for building both athletic skills and lifelong friendships.
