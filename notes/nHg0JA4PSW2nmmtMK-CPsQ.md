> 原轉會各民族代表聯署聲明

Joint Declaration by the Representatives of the Indigenous Peoples of Taiwan Serving on the Indigenous Historical Justice and Transitional Justice Committee

### 習近平先生，你不認識我們，因此你不認識台灣。

### Mr. Xi Jinping: you do not know us, so you do not know Taiwan.

> 我們是台灣原住民族，生存在台灣這片母親土地上超過六千年，不是中華民族中的少數民族。我們祖先在台灣玉山、阿里山、大霸尖山、大武山、卑南主山、都蘭山以及山林、草原、深谷、溪流、島嶼、海洋流傳的故事明白顯示：台灣是原住民族的傳統領域，也是原住民族世世代代用生命守護的祖靈土地，不是中國的領土。

*We, the indigenous peoples of Taiwan, have lived in Taiwan, our Motherland, for more than six thousand years. We are NOT "ethnic minorities of the Chinese nation". As evidenced by the stories passed down to us by our ancestors of the mountains, forests, grasslands, valleys, creeks, islands, and oceanic waters, Taiwan has long been the traditional territory of this land’s indigenous peoples. Generations of indigenous peoples have committed their lives guarding the land blessed by the spirits of our ancestors. Taiwan does not belong to China. 

> 台灣原住民族見證著來到島嶼的西班牙人、荷蘭人、鄭氏王朝、清帝國、日本國、中華民國的言行，我們跟荷蘭人簽過契約、跟美國人簽過和平協議，對抗過每一個侵略我們土地的外來民族與帝國主義，也受到後來殖民國家的武力鎮壓、威權統治，從被稱番人到台灣原來的主人，原住民族更推動著國家走上人權、民主、自由的轉變歷程。千百年來，我們仍在這裡，從未放棄我們的自然主權。

We, the indigenous peoples of Taiwan, have for centuries witnessed the deeds and empty words of those who have pushed up onto our island’s shores: the Spanish, the Dutch, the Kingdom of Tungning/the Zheng Kingdom, the Qing Empire, the Japanese, and the Republic of China. We have had contracts with the Dutch and peace agreements with the American; along the way we resisted the foreign nations and imperialism which invaded our land, but eventually had to endure the military repression and authoritarian ruling of the colonists. We were called savages but now we are recognized as Taiwan’s original occupants. The indigenous peoples of Taiwan have helped propel the transition of this country towards human rights, democracy, and freedom. After thousands of years, here is where we stand. We have never given up our inherent sovereign power over the land of Taiwan.

### 習近平先生，你不懂尊嚴，因此誤會了偉大。

### Mr. Xi Jinping: you do not understand dignity, so you misunderstand greatness.

> 在我們母親土地上建立起來的主權國家台灣，我們並不滿意，因為原住民族的歷史正義與轉型正義才剛剛開始被這個國家重視，台灣島上的多元民族、多元文化、多元歷史觀才剛剛開始受到這個國家肯定。但，這也是我們與所有認同台灣土地的其他族群努力形塑的國家，是不同族群正在理解彼此痛苦經歷的國家，是我們可以大聲用自己語言說自己故事的國家。我們在母親土地上自己決定想要什麼樣的國家，並積極改造它，這是尊嚴。無論人口數是300多人的卡那卡那富族，還是21萬多人的阿美族，我們每一個原住民族都有平等的自決權，這是尊嚴。
> 

We are not content with the status quo of Taiwan, the sovereign state build upon our Motherland, because the historical justice and transitional justice of the indigenous peoples have only recently begun to be respected. This State has begun to recognize the ethnic and cultural diversity of its peoples and their diverse perspectives of history as well. Nevertheless, Taiwan is a nation that we are all still striving to build together, along with other peoples who recognize this land for what it actually is. 

This is a nation where different groups of people are trying to understand each other’s painful historical experiences. This is the land where we can proudly tell our own stories in our own languages. We are peoples with dignity. Dignity means that we enjoy the freedom to shape the country according to our aspirations and work hard to improve it. Dignity also means each and every indigenous people has the equal right to self-determination, may it be the Kanakanavu Tribe of 300 some members or the  Amis with a population more than 210,000.

> 習近平先生代表中國政府所推銷的單一文化價值、統一、強權，並不偉大也不令人嚮往。對土地謙卑、尊重其他生命、與各族群共存共好，才是我們的信念。

What Mr. Xi promotes on behalf of China - mono-culturalism, inter-country unification, and hegemonism - is neither great nor appealing. Instead, these are our believes: to be humble to the lands, to respect other lives, and to co-exist with other peoples and populations for the common good.

### 習近平先生，人不應該傷害別人，無論這個人跟自己多麼不一樣。

### Mr. Xi Jinping: no one should harm other people, no matter how different they are.

> 習先生代表中國政府的談話，強調以武力為後盾，堅持統一台灣、實施一國兩制，並表示不會傷害同為中國人的人。但暴力是不對的，無論是不是中國人，都不應該遭到傷害。我們已經看到藏族、維吾爾族成為「中國人」之後，在「民族自治區」遭遇文化、語言、信仰的滅絕。我們看到一國兩制之下的香港人民，快速失去民主與自由。我們看到中國人民，甚至無法說出或捍衛自己的基本人權。

As the representative of China’s government, Mr. Xi in his recent speech insisted on unifying Taiwan and implementing the “One Country Two Systems” policy. Mr. Xi said this would be backed by China’s military force, which will not be used against people who are Chinese. However, violence of any kind is wrong, whether it is used to harm Chinese or other people. 

We have witnessed how the Tibetan and the Uyghur peoples have been driven into cultural, linguistic, and religious extinction in the “ethnic minority autonomous regions” after they were forced to become “Chinese". We have witnessed how Hong Kong people, under this “One Country Two Systems” framework, have experienced rapid erosion and loss of their democracy and freedoms. We have witnessed that China’s own people cannot speak up for, let alone defend, their fundamental human rights. 

> 習先生，暴力無法帶來和平，Misawacu hanizaay masasu takid（欺侮別人的人也會受到同樣的報應；撒奇萊雅族古諺）。請帶領你的國家邁向真正的文明，停止武力恫嚇台灣人民，致力讓中國人民享有人權與自由。

Mr. Xi: Violence never leads to peace. An old *Sakizaya* saying says *misawacu hanizaay masasu takid*, which means “Those who bully others will have the same brought back to them.” Please lead your country to true civilization, stop intimidating Taiwanese people with threats of force, and strive to bring human rights and freedom to the Chinese people.

### 習近平先生，台灣原住民族與台灣的主體性，拒絕威脅也不退讓。

### Mr. Xi Jinping: The inherent sovereignty of Taiwan’s indigenous peoples and the sovereignty of Taiwan will not be threatened, and we will not recede.

> 台灣的國家未來，是母親土地上所有族群的自主決定，包括台灣原住民族在內。同時，沒有經過原住民族行使集體自決權之前，任何政府、政黨、團體都不得與外來勢力及國家協商，將原住民族的傳統領域併入他國領土或成為他國實質管理的範圍。這是我們守護母親土地的決心，台灣原住民族堅持了數千年，也會繼續堅持下去。

The future of Taiwan as a State shall be determined by the free will of all its populations and ethnic groups, including the indigenous peoples. Without collective self-determination by the indigenous peoples, no government, political party, or group shall negotiate with any foreign power or state for the incorporation of indigenous tranditional territory to be annexed into another country or to be controlled de facto by any other country. We commit ourselves to defending our Motherland. The indigenous peoples of Taiwan have persevered for thousands of years and will continue to do so.


> 如果有一天，中國放棄扭曲的歷史觀、民族觀、國家觀，樂意成為我們的善良鄰居，而不是強行要當我們的父母。那個時候，我們會誠心舉杯，敬中國這個鄰居一杯小米酒。pasola xmnx na mansonsou!（願您每回呼吸都順暢；鄒族語）

If some day China should cast aside its distorted understanding of history, nation, and modern-day statehood and be willing to turn itself into a friendly neighbor, instead of proclaiming to be our "parent" — only then will we heartily make a toast to China, our neighbor, with a glass of millet wine. *pasola xmnx na mansonsou!* (“May every breath you take a smooth one.” — *Tsou*)
