My speaking improved dramatically after I committed to daily practice sessions. Before I would study English whenever I had time which meant sometimes going weeks without speaking at all. Four months ago I started using [learn english with ai](https://promova.com/page/practice-with-ai) for twenty minutes every single morning and the consistency changed everything. My accent got clearer and I stopped pausing to think of words mid sentence. The AI adapts to your mistakes so you work on exactly what you need. Some days I didn't feel motivated but forced myself anyway and those sessions mattered most. If you only take one thing from my experience it's this: practice every day no matter what. Skip a day and your progress slows down noticeably
