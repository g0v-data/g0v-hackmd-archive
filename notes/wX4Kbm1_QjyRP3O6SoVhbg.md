What are the club hours in Miami?

Miami is famous for its vibrant nightlife, offering a mix of rooftop lounges, pool bars, and legendary nightclubs. For those looking to enjoy a night out, club hours in Miami generally range from late evenings until the early hours of the morning. Most clubs open around 10 PM and stay alive until 3 AM to 5 AM, depending on the venue and the day of the week. Fridays and Saturdays typically have extended hours, as they are peak nights for nightlife enthusiasts.
