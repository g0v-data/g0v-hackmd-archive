Summaries from Sep 13 2025

1. Certification of hardware
Japanese law requires new models of devices that transmit radio frequency need to be certified by government standards
Can only apply be organizations and take long time
Ways to work around - temporary (evaluation license) 3 month, but still difficult
This can be a problem for open hardware projects for building mesh networks, . 

2. Digital government 
Initiative for all governments to store data in government cloud 
Digital Agency has negotiated collective contracts for cloud services. 
But the implementation and operation still need to sign another contract, difficult to find out how much other local government paid
Some kind of Collective Procurement Project may help - 

3. To start a website in Korean, there are many guidelines to follow. To make things easier for small operators, PIPC in Korea provide a template (still read carefully before adopting)
