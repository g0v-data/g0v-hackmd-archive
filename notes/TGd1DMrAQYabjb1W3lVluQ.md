## Distribution
Principles and Instructions
- This Hypercerts is designed to maintain a smooth community operation and fellowship distribution, so the allocation principles will be based on the job content, contribution and level of responsibility of each position.
    - The Director will in charge of the overall direction of Frontline Fellowship and will be accountable for all programs and decisions.
    - The Infrastructure Architect will be responsible for the website design and maintenance of the information database system, as well as the poster design for each workshop or launch.
    - The Content Editor will be responsible for all content writing and will assist in revising the articles of the awardees.
    - The Community Facilitator will hold on to the whole community operations, which is an open ecosystem assembling with writers, editors, researchers, media organisations, agencies and more.
