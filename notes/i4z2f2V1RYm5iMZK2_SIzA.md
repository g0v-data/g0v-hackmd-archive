# Rebuilding trust online / 重建網路互信

## The project: / 計畫內容：

Modern digital communications have a powerful effect on social trust and relationships. Some online platforms are breaking trust between individuals, groups and institutions, in part by rewarding extreme views and elevating misinformation.
現代數位通訊對社會互信與社會關係的影響強而有力。某些網路平台正在破壞個人、團體與制度間的互信，有部分係以獎勵極端見解與散播錯誤訊息為手段。

This project examines how new online systems and platforms can help us rebuild trust to keep individuals and society strong.
本計畫旨在調查新的網路系統與平台如何幫助我們重建信任，以維持強健的個人與社會。

Learnings will appear in a book to be published globally by Penguin.  
調查結果將會呈現在書中，由企鵝出版社在全球發行。

## Goal: Learn about digital initiatives that nurture trust between individuals, groups and institutions. / 目標：瞭解促進個人、團體與制度間互信的數位倡議


### The problem: Antisocial media / 難題：反社會媒體

Existing social media platforms are useful. But they have enabled the spread of disinformation and encouraged societal polarization in many countries. These adverse outcomes occur in part because social media depend on surveilance for advertising revenue, and in part because their reward systems (such as likes and shares) incentivize and amplify more extreme content.  
現有的社群媒體甚有用處，卻也使假訊息得以散播，並助長許多國家社會極化。這些不利的結果一方面肇因於社群媒體仰賴其對廣告收入的監測，另一方面則是其獎勵機制（如按讚和分享）助長了更加極端的內容。

### The goal: Learn solutions from g0v participants / 目標：從g0v參與者探究解決方法

My goal is to learn specific digital initiatives that nurture trust between individuals, groups and insitutions. These might include:
我的目標是探究促進個人、團體與制度間互信的數位倡議，其中包括：

* social media platforms or hacks for existing ones that support more kindness and less polarization. (Example: [Front Porch Forum](https://frontporchforum.com/media/article68), a neighbourhood-connection app with a slow interface.)
鼓勵更多善意和減少極化的社群媒體或現有平台的黑客。 (例如：[Front Porch Forum](https://frontporchforum.com/media/article68) 是一個用來連結街坊鄰居的app。）
###
* initiatives that fight the spread of disinformation online.(Example: [this story](https://www.dw.com/en/how-taiwan-is-countering-chinese-disinformation/a-62931086) mentions that Cofacts is collaborating with government to fight disinformation. Or the Taiwan Fact Check Centre's add-on for Facebook [here](https://tfc-taiwan.org.tw/topic/4112).) 
對抗散布假訊息的倡議。（例如：[這篇報導](https://www.dw.com/en/how-taiwan-is-countering-chinese-disinformation/a-62931086) 提到 Cofacts 正在與政府合作打擊假訊息。或是台灣事實查核中心的 [Facebook 附加元件](https://tfc-taiwan.org.tw/topic/4112)。)
> This is not an accurate description of Cofacts. [name=chihao]

###
* initiatives that create trust between people and government through collaboration and transparency.(Example：[Polis](https://pol.is/home).)
藉由協作和透明化建立官民互信的倡議。（例如：[Polis](https://pol.is/home)。)
