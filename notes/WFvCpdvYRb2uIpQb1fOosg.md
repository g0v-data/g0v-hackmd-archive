*Owing to the Covid19 pandemic, requiremets of telehealth and online psychological care services increase rapidly. G0V civic hackers know the importance of mental wellbeing in the world crisis and initiated #psymap project to create an online information platform, collecting goverment and non-government telehealth information like hotlines, online counselling services, online menatal health consultations, healthcare app, webcam psychiatric enquiry, self-care kit resources etc. The #psymap project cross-validates the data with the official list of 180 psychiatric clinics which provides telehealth services and 210 hospitals. The #psymap project updates in daily bases.
