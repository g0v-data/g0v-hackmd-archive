### Switch 電子開關
1. **A Single n type transistor (Working Transistor)**
- source => negatively-charged, sit on a positively-charged p silicon
- gate
- drain => negatively-charged, sit on a positively-charged p silicon 
    - ON
        - ==positive voltage== is applied to ++the gate++ and ++the drain++
        - ==form an electron channel== between ++the source++ and ++the drain++
    - OFF
        - remove the voltage
