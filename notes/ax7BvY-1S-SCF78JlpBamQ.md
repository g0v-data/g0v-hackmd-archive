### Challenge in Nigeria
- Lack of public participation in the legislature structure 
- National Assembly Public Hearing is not accessible for many citizens 
    - Federal lesgislature: Nigeria has 36 states and federal level of the government. National Assembly has two chambers and serves as the legislature agency 
    - Not an open house, no transparency even in the public hearing 
- Goal: Renewal of public hearing system 
- How is the space in state level: no concern for National Assembly 
    - exculsive list of lawmaking design in Constitutional Law
