Event to be held at the following time, date and location:

Friday, 7 September 2018 at 22:30

Brucknerhaus (Mittlerer Saal)
Untere Donaulände 7
Mittlerer Saal is next to the main hall of the GALA
4020 Linz
Austria

[View Map](http://maps.google.com/maps?q=Untere+Donaul%C3%A4nde+7,+Linz,++4020+Austria&hl=en)

Prix & STARTS Winners Salon

Ars Electronica warmly invites you to the Prix & STARTS Winners Salon:  a casual reception, where all artists who received an honorary mention or an award of Distinction at Prix Ars Electronica 2018 and STARTS PRIZE 2018 will be honored with a prize certificate. Come relax, unwind and enjoy a drink after the day´s various activities, to share experiences and meet with this year’s Prix Ars Electronica & STARTS PRIZE Winners.

We look foward to welcoming you at the Prix & STARTS Winners Salon!
