CS2 has its own way of handling skins and items that is worth understanding before spending anything on the market. Cases are the main source of new skins entering the game but you can also get basic weapon drops just from playing regularly. Before going further into the market side of things it is worth taking some time to [learn more](https://dmarket.com/cs2-wiki/) through the DMarket CS2 wiki which covers float values, wear conditions and how pricing actually works. Two listings of the same skin can look similar but carry very different prices and that catches most newer players off guard. Prices shift based on supply, condition, and how popular a particular finish is at any given time so having a solid understanding of the basics before making any purchases makes a real difference
