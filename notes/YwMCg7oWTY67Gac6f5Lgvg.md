### Defining da0 Mission & Purpose

Currently listed possible mission statements
- From deck to PL
    - ***Supercharge g0v’s success in civic innovation with web3***
        - achieve g0v’s governance & impact goals
        - field of experimentation for PL
        - export knowledge & learning globally
    - Initiatives that can potentially help g0v
        - Impact certificates for project funding (ex. jothon, cofacts, anyone else)
        - DID & DeSoc (work with Gitcoin or develop our own)
        - Quadratic funding (possibility to work with Gitcoin 2.9)

- From Noah's conversation with Jack
    - ***the world's leading DeSoc***
- From pure imagination
    - ***da0 是正面循環社會的第零層***
