# Code for Japan open-legislation vTaiwan workshop（2019/3/9-10)

[報名請按我！！！](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfxsCPThdNRvcVXCp10rNgTg8utKuLpOjNDggYsbjA0dFlEDw/viewform)

## 緣起說明
Code for Japan 詢問 vTaiwan 能否就參與式立法合作申請Code for All 的計畫，協助 NGO Pinka 推動日本的參與式立法，希望邀 vTaiwan 在日本舉辦 workshop。

## 概要
- 日期確定：2019/3/9-10
- [議程規劃（歡迎提供建議）](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1T6YMKW1S78c6aHazgL5adokZ8ngMUPubuP-7bT5xD0Y/edit?usp=sharing)

【DAY 1 @ Kasumigaseki 】
1) Share vTaiwan's process and experience (80min, from vTaiwan, g0v) 
2) Open Legislation Collaboration Exercise (170min) 

Theme
At the era of digitization, online identity verification(KYC) is essential for receiving public and private services. However, accurate KYC also impose users some procedures and there is ”trade-off" between "convenience and comfort" and "safety and security”. For making good-balanced rules, we discuss at the multi - stakeholder about whether we can offer.

Time table
* 13:00-13:10 opening
* 13:10-14:30 Share vTaiwan's process and experience
* 14:40-17:30 Open Legislation Collaboration Exercise
* 17:30-17:40 closing

Participating members:
Citizens, Companies, Cabinet Secretariat, Ministry of Internal Affairs

【DAY 2 @ Kamakura-city】
1) Share vTaiwan's process and experience (80min, from vTaiwan, g0v) 
2) Open Legislation Collaboration Exercise (170min) 

Theme
Recently people's lifestyles and ways of thinking about it are undergoing a major shift. We need reforms of how to work and Telework is attracting attention as one of solutions. Kamakura-city launched Telework / Lifestyle Study Group Preparatory Meeting for preparing the environment of telework there. In this time, we will discuss how to promote public opinion, environment improvement, institution design by the multi - stakeholder.

Time table
* 13:00-13:10 opening
* 13:10-14:30 Share vTaiwan's process and experience
* 14:40-17:30 Open Legislation Collaboration Exercise
* 17:30-17:40 closing

Participating members
Citizens, Companies, Kamakura City Hall
