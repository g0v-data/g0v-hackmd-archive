The geocomposites market is poised for significant growth in the foreseeable future, driven by several factors. Another growth driver is the rising demand from the mining and agriculture industries. Geocomposites play vital roles in controlling acid mine drainage, preventing soil erosion, and enhancing soil and rock structures in mining, while in agriculture, they aid in soil stabilization, erosion control, and drainage. As the demand for minerals and food production rises, the need for geocomposites in these sectors is expected to grow.

**Segment Analysis**

Based on application, the market is categorized into water and wastewater management, road and highway, dams and riverbanks, landfill and mining, soil reinforcement for civil construction, and others.

The road & highway segment held the largest market share in 2023 and is projected to maintain its dominance in the forecast period. Geocomposites play a crucial role in soil reinforcement for civil construction, particularly in various road and highway applications such as subgrade separation and stabilization, base reinforcement, overlay stress absorption, and overlay reinforcement. They enhance soil stability, prevent erosion, and facilitate drainage. Geocomposites also provide a separation function, preserving the integrity and functionality of different layers in road construction, thereby enhancing performance and longevity of road infrastructure.
