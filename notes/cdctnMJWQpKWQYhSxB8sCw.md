## 專案簡介 Intro

由 g0v 社群參與者發起，《零時檔案局》要用開源鄉民的力量，蒐集資料，協力研究台灣資訊空間。

0archive is a g0v project to archive and analyze Taiwan’s information space with open-source, automated tools, producing open-source data sets and reports.

《零時檔案局》是⋯

1. 開源、自動的資料蒐集軟體系統；
2. 開源、開放的資料集；
3. 交換資料標準格式；
4. 主辦小松，餵養資料分析和詮釋的社群；
5. 各式社群生產的內容及報告。

0archive is…
1. an open-source, automated archiving system consists of both general-use and language-specific tools to crawl, parse, format, and archive text and visual content from various online sources into open data sets;
2. a collection of said data sets;
3. the data standards and schemas for the storage and exchange of said data sets;
4. a community focusing on analyzing and interpreting said data sets; and
5. reports and other types of published content for public engagement produced by said communities using said data sets and tools.
