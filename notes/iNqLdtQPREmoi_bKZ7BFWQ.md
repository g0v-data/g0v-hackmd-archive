## Choosing the Right Diamond Proportions

Not all radiant cut diamonds have the same appearance.

Some are nearly square, while others have a more elongated rectangular shape.

Square radiant cuts create a balanced, symmetrical look, while elongated versions make fingers appear longer and provide greater visual coverage.

When selecting your diamond, consider:

* Length-to-width ratio
* Table percentage
* Depth percentage
* Symmetry
* Polish

These factors contribute to both brilliance and overall appearance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is a radiant cut diamond more brilliant than an emerald cut?

Yes. Radiant cut diamonds feature brilliant-cut facets that produce significantly more sparkle than the step-cut facets of an emerald cut.

### Is a 2-carat radiant cut diamond a good size?

Yes. A two-carat radiant cut offers impressive finger coverage, exceptional brilliance, and a luxurious appearance suitable for many engagement ring styles.

### Are lab-grown radiant cut diamonds worth buying?

Absolutely. They provide the same beauty, durability, and sparkle as natural diamonds while often offering better value.

### Which metal looks best with a radiant cut diamond?

White gold and platinum enhance brilliance, while yellow and rose gold create warm contrast. The best choice depends on your personal style.

### Does radiant cut hide inclusions well?

Yes. The brilliant facet pattern helps conceal many small inclusions, making radiant cut diamonds an excellent choice for balancing beauty and value.
