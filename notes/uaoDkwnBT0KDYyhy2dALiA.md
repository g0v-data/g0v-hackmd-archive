**Shift towards natural and organic ingredients drives market growth.**

One of the major drivers of the active ingredients market is the shift towards natural and organic ingredients. The national health and environmental concerns induced by the use of synthetic ingredients also fuel the trend, contemplating the industry's development derived from nature. Also, with the emergence of various regulatory frameworks such as the EU Cosmetics Regulation, which aims at promoting transparency and safety in the usage of natural ingredients, manufacturers have been propelled to be transparent and include detailed labeling of products. The force facilitates consumer knowledge and trust in branded companies, which also drives market growth. Hence, manufacturers are focusing on developing natural active ingredients for the market, which meet consumer needs and comply with the regulations.

**Segment Analysis**

**By Functionality**

Conditioning agents are the leaders of the functionality sector being responsible for more than 35% of the revenue in 2023. The popularity and use of this type of active ingredient are determined by the fact that they play an important role in many industries. Agents are responsible for the improvement of product performance in the cases when they enhance their texture, appearance, and quality. The agents are used for the preparation of many personal care items and household cleaners, and their high efficiency and value make them necessary components that ensure the agent's leading position.

**By Application**

In 2023, the largest revenue share is held by Skin Care, by Application. This is due to an increasing consumer awareness regarding the benefits of following a skin care routine and the growing demand for effective skin care products. The rising concerns regarding aging, pollution and UV radiation are also driving the need for advanced cosmetic formulations. Additionally, frequent innovations in active ingredients dedicated to treating specific skin related problems are positively impacting the dominance of this segment in the market.
