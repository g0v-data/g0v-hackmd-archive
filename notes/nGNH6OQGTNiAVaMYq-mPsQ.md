# 學生參與者與他們的產地 學生參與觀察報告

## 議程資訊

- 主題：FtO Jeju 2023 學生參與觀察報告 / FtO Jeju 2023 Student Participation Observation Report
- 時長：50 分鐘
- 講者：Ted 顥天、Sky 軾凱、Astrid 柚子、Chen-Wei 宸維
- 說明：以參與 FtO Jeju 2023 夥伴為主，因於參與過程進行學生社群參與觀察並撰寫相關報告，以此主題帶動有關學生參與情形之討論
- 議程簡介
    - 「面海松」Facing the Ocean Meet & Hack 是由生活在西太平洋的公民黑客組成的社群「FtO 面向海洋」自 2019 年開始舉辦的黑客松系列活動。在美國國際民主協會補助下，g0v、Code for Japan 及 Code for Korea 各有幾位學生獲補助參與 FtO Jeju 2023，並進一步觀察三個國家之間的學生在公民科技當中的參與經驗與狀態。本議程邀請到 2023 的代表學生 Ted 顥天、Sky 軾凱、Astrid 柚子、Chen-Wei 宸薇，分享他們在過程中所觀察到對「如何促進學生參與公民科技」之發現。
    - "Facing the Ocean Meet & Hack" is a series of hackathons initiated by the community FtO composed of civic hackers living in the Western Pacific since 2019. With funding from the National Democratic Institute, several students from g0v, Code for Japan, and Code for Korea were granted subsidies to participate in FtO Jeju 2023, facilitating further observation of students' participation experiences and status in civic technology across the three countries. This session invites representatives from 2023, including students Ted, Sky, Astrid, and Chen-Wei, to share their findings on how to promote student participation in civic technology based on their observations.
