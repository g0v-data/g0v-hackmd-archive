<h2>Why Do Small Letters Need Special Care?</h2>

<p>Small letters need special care because thread has thickness. If letters are too tiny, the thread can close the open spaces and make the text hard to read.</p>

<p>That is why the best embroidery digitizing service checks letter height before digitizing. The digitizer may use a clearer font, more spacing, or a slightly larger size. Small changes can bring big relief when the logo is for uniforms or staff shirts.</p>

<h2>How Does Stitch Density Affect Clean Vector Art?</h2>

<p>Stitch density controls how close the stitches sit together. Too much density makes the design thick and stiff. Too little density can leave gaps in the fabric.</p>

<p>The best embroidery digitizing services adjust density by fabric type. A soft shirt, thick jacket, and structured cap do not need the same setup. This careful balance helps the final design look clean, not heavy or rough.</p>
