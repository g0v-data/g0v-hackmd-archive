# Full Details 完整說明

## Summary 概述
> NASA’s near-term space exploration plans include returning to the Moon and future plans include landing a crew on Mars. Your challenge is to create an inspiring and educational video game or interactive 3D model to simulate a landing on the Moon or Mars!

美國國家航空暨太空總署(NASA)近期的太空探索計劃包括重返月球，且在未來讓太空人登陸火星。你的挑戰是創建一款振奮人心並有教育意義的遊戲或交互式3D模型，來模擬登入月球或火星。

## Details 詳細描述
### Background 背景說明
> NASA’s near-term space exploration plans include returning to the Moon and future plans include landing a crew on Mars. The Agency supports efforts to inform and educate the public about these important endeavors. Publicly available information about NASA's Artemis Program and the Apollo Lunar Excursion Module can provide data required to address this challenge, including information that will inspire the public to learn more about the NASA Artemis program and NASA history.

美國太空總署近期的太空探索計劃包括重返月球，且在未來讓太空人登陸火星。此機構對於向普羅大眾宣傳這些重要成就大力支持。從美國太空總署阿特米絲計劃和阿波羅登月艙的公開資訊可以得到應對這一挑戰所需的數據，例如激發大眾進一步了解美國太空總署阿特米絲計劃和美國太空總署歷史的相關資訊。

### Objectives 目標
> Your challenge is to create an inspiring and educational video game web application or interactive 3D model to simulate a landing on the Moon or Mars! When developing the video game or 3D model, think about how it could educate or inspire the player.
> 
> Products from this challenge should be deployed as web pages that include embedded web apps. The web page may provide instructions on how to use the web app.
> 
> If you reuse free open-source assets or content released under a Creative Commons license (such as artwork, models, code bases, or textures), comply with all appropriate requirements.

你的挑戰是創建一款振奮人心並有教育意義的遊戲或交互式3D模型，來模擬登陸月球或火星。當你在設計此款遊戲或是3D模型時，請思考看看你的作品可以如何教育或是鼓勵玩家。

本挑戰的作品應以包含嵌入式網路應用程式的網頁來呈現。網頁可提供關於如何使用該網路應用程式的說明。

如果你使用免費的開放資產或以知識共享協議發布的內容（如藝術品、模型、代碼庫或紋理），作品須符合該協議之規範。

### Potential Considerations 潛在考量
> As you develop your video game web application or interactive 3D model, you may (but are not required to) consider the following:
> 
> 1. Depending on your team's level of experience, your video game could range from an “old school” 2D side scroller to a first-person WebVR experience:
    >  - Old school 2D video game - a profile of hills and craters scroll by as you control a lander with arrow keys to find and land on a surface that is relatively flat. Teams with intermediate programming experience could implement physics equations to create a more realistic simulator.
    >  - First-person WebVR experience - a first-person game could be implemented with a game engine that provides some physics simulation of gravity. Research the gravity of the Moon or Mars to adjust the parameters for more realism. Teams with intermediate experience may want to develop or apply code to procedurally generate a rough surface. Moon Trek and Mars Trek (see Example Resources) are NASA web apps that enable you to download a small section of the 3D model of the Lunar or Martian surface.
    >  
> 2. Objectives of a video game could be exploration, scientific, or commercial. For example, an exploration game could present a fuel gauge indicating near-empty status or a low propellant supply when the nearby surface is not suitable for landing. The player would then need to find a good place to land before the fuel or propellant runs out. A game with a science-driven objective could simulate a hopper that the player could maneuver to visit multiple sites to collect samples. A game with a commercial objective could involve planning the delivery of multiple payloads to the surface and successful delivery could result in payment. Development of surface infrastructure will require the delivery of heavy equipment and there could be multiple companies delivering payloads; so, a strategy-based game could involve placement of equipment to enable infrastructure development. 
> 3. If your team is more interested in design and visualization, consider developing an interactive 3D model of a planetary lander for humans, robots, or cargo and embed the model(s) into a web page. The Graphics Language Transfer Format (GLTF) or binary GLTF (GLB) models can be embedded in a web page. A model could present an exterior view of a lander on a surface, the interior, or both.
> 4. Potential keyword search terms to find additional resources include: tutorial to embed 3D model in a web page, free open source game development, 3D JavaScript game engine, code repository, creative commons. Inclusion in this list of search terms does not constitute an endorsement.
> 
> For data and resources related to this challenge, refer to the Resources tab at the top of the page. More resources may be added before the hackathon begins. NASA does not endorse any non-U.S. Government entity and is not responsible for information contained on non-U.S. Government websites.


當你開發遊戲應用程式或交互式3D模型時，你可以（但不是必須）考慮以下幾點：
* 根據你的團隊經驗，你的遊戲可以是一個經典的2D卷軸動作遊戲，也可以是個第一人稱WebVR體驗。
    * 經典2D遊戲--當你用方向鍵控制著陸器，找到並降落在一個相對平坦的表面時，山丘和隕石坑的輪廓就會慢慢逼近。中等程度的團隊可以使用物理相關程式，創造一個更真實的模擬器。
    * 第一人稱WebVR體驗--第一人稱遊戲可以透過提供一些物理學模擬重力的遊戲來呈現。研究月球或火星的重力，調整參數以獲得更多的真實感。有中等程度的團隊可以發展及應用各式程式來產生一個粗糙的表面。 你可以從美國太空總署的網路應用程式，如Moon Trek和Mars Trek（詳細請參考範例資源），輕鬆下載月球或火星表面3D模型的一小部分。

* 遊戲的目標可以是科學性質或商業性質的探險。例如，可以在附近的表面不適合著陸時，呈現出燃料表顯示接近耗盡的狀態或推進劑供應不足。然後，玩家需要在燃料或推進劑耗盡之前找到一個好地方降落。一個以科學探險為目標的遊戲可以模擬一個跳板，玩家可以透過跳板造訪多個地點來收集樣本。一個以商業探險為目標的遊戲可能涉及到計劃將多個酬載送到地面，成功運送即可得到款項。地面基礎設施的發展將會需要運送重型設備，也可能有多家公司想要運送酬載；因此，此類策略遊戲可以涉及設備放置位置，以實現基礎設施的發展。
* 如果你的團隊對設計和視覺化等方面更感興趣，可以考慮為人類、機器人或貨物開發一個交互式行星登陸器的3D模型，並將模型嵌入到網頁中。圖形語言傳輸格式（GLTF）或二進制GLTF（GLB）模型可以嵌入於網頁中。一個模型可以展示著陸器的表面、內部或兩者兼備。
* 若需搜尋其他相關資源，關鍵字包括：在網頁中嵌入3D模型的教學、免費的開放遊戲開發、3D JavaScript遊戲引擎、代碼庫、創意共享。列入這些搜索詞，並沒有加分效果。

有關與這項挑戰相關的數據與資源，請參考頁面頂端的資源標籤（Resources）。在黑客松競賽開始前，會加入更多資源。美國太空總署並不會替任何非美國政府的機構背書，也不對非美國政府網站上的資訊負責。
