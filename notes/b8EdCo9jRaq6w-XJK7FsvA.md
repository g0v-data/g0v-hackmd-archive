Zhòngdiǎn zài tígōng `shí lián zhì'.`Jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì'xìtǒng shì shí lián zhì qízhōng yīzhǒng fāngshì, yǔ zhǐ bǐ dēngjì jí gè diànjiā zìxíng zhìzuò biǎodān bìng bù chōngtú. Zhèngfǔ bù huì qiángzhì shǐyòng zhège xìtǒng. Cǐ xìtǒng biànyú yì diào zhuīzōng guǐjī, kěyǐ zài jiāngù xiàolǜ zhī yú tóngshí quèbǎo zīxùn zhèngquè xìng, jiànyì dàjiā duōduō shǐyòng. ## Shēnqǐng zhège yào qián ma? Cǐ xìtǒng wánquán miǎnfèi, qǐng ānxīn shǐyòng. ## Yào zěnme shēnqǐng gěi diànjiāyòng de QRcode? 5/19 Xiàwǔ sān diǎn kāifàng shēnqǐng. 1. Jìnrù `[jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì chǎngsuǒ QR Code dēngjì zuòyè](https://Emask.Taiwan.Gov.Tw/real/)'xìtǒng. 2. Tígōng tǒng biān huò shēnfèn zhèng hào, shǒujī hàomǎ, quèrèn hòu jí kě huòdé bāohán chǎngsuǒ dàimǎ de QRcode. Chǎngsuǒ dàimǎ suíjī chǎnshēng, bù bāohán shāngyè dēngjì huò dìdiǎn zīxùn. Rènhé zhǒnglèi de jīguān, dānwèi, gèrén yíngyèzhě, dōu kě suíshí shēnqǐng xīn de QRcode. ## Qǐngwèn fēndiàn rúhé chǎnshēng ne? Shì bào zǒng gōngsī tǒng biān ma? Háishì diàn hào? Zǒng gōngsī kěyǐ chǎnshēng gè fēndiàn de QR Code, huò shì shòuquán gè fēndiàn bǎ zǒng gōngsī de diàn mǎ hòumiàn zìxíng jiā shàng fēndiàn biānhào (zhǐ néng yòng shùzì) zài shǒudòng chǎnshēng QR Code. ## Wǒ zhīqián méiyǒu shǐyòngguò `kǒuzhào shímíngzhì'yùgòu xìtǒng, yào rúhé qǔdé QRcode? Tòuguò `shǒujī rènzhèng', huò `jiànbǎo kuài yì tōng',`TW FidO',`jiànbǎo kǎ',`zìránrén píngzhèng'děng duōyuán fāngshì, jūn kě dēngrù. ## Ruò mínzhòng tiánxiě fēi zìjǐ de diànhuà hàomǎ, diànjiā rúhé quèrèn? Mínzhòng bùyòng tiánxiě diànhuà hàomǎ, ér shì shǐyòng zìjǐ de shǒujī sǎo QRcode fāsòng jiǎnxùn zhì 1922, bìng chūshì jiǎnxùn fāsòng wánchéng dehuàmiàn gěi gāi chǎngsuǒ rényuán,1922 huì jìlù fā jiǎnxùn de shǒujī hàomǎ. ## Wǒ shì liúdòng tānfàn,QRcode yào tiē nǎlǐ? Kèrén lái bu huì dài shǒujī zěnme bàn? Cǐ xìtǒng shì shí lián zhì qízhōng yīzhǒng fāngshì, yǔ zhǐ bǐ dēngjì jí gè diànjiā zìxíng zhìzuò biǎodān bìng bù chōngtú, rúguǒ yǒu nányǐ cǎi xíng zhī chù, réng kě xuǎnzé qítā zuòfǎ. ## Rúguǒ wǒ de QR Code bèi dàoyòng bìng zhāngtiē zài qítā de wèizhì, gāi zěnme bàn? Rúguǒ fāxiàn zhèyàng de qíngkuàng, qǐng tōngzhī 1922 jí kě. ## Rúguǒ tuījiàn mínzhòng ānzhuāng `táiwān shèjiāo jùlí'App, shìfǒu kěyǐ qǔdài jiǎnxùn? Huānyíng tuījiàn ānzhuāng `táiwān shèjiāo jùlí'App, dàn xū shīxíng shí lián zhì de diànjiā, réng xū tòuguò bāohán `jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì'xìtǒng zài nèi děng rènhé fāngshì dáchéng. (Yīzhào shí lián zhì cuòshī zhǐyǐn, táiwān shèjiāo jùlí App mùqián wúfǎ tígōng yì diào shí bìyào de liánluò zīxùn.) ## Xiànzài yǒuxiē gōngsī xíng hào huò jūzhù shèqū, tāmen zài yǒu mínzhòng lái yěshì yào shímíngzhì, nà tāmen yào xuǎnzé shénme shēnfèn?(0519 Jìzhě huì yāng guǎng tíwèn) fǎrén hé gèrén de diànjiā zhǔyào chābié jiùshì yǒuwú tǒng biān, ruò yǒu, jiù yòng fǎrén diànjiā, ruò méiyǒu, xiàng gōngyù dàshà, zhǐyào yǒu dàibiǎo rén jiù kěyǐ yòng gèrén diànjiā lái shēnqǐng. ## Zhè kuǎn app, jiǎnxùn chuán chū qū shìfǒu yǒu shèdìng zīliào liú duōjiǔ gēn duōjiǔ shānchú, xiànzài yǒu xū duō diànjiā dōu shì shǐyòng wèifú bù shèjì de google biǎodān, cǐ biǎodān wèilái hái yǒu shǐyòng ma? Háishì kǎolǜ hūyù shǐyòng xiànzài de app.(0519 Jìzhě huì TVBS tíwèn) zhè bùshì app, suǒyǐ méiyǒu cúnzài shǒujī duōjiǔ de wèntí, chuán chūqù de jiǎnxùn běnlái jiù huì liú zài shǒujī lǐ, shān liú yǔ fǒu qǔjué yú měi gèrén. Jiǎnxùn chéngshì huì liú xià de jìlù zhǐyǒu chǎngsuǒ dàimǎ, bù huì yǒu diànjiā dìzhǐ, jīngwěidù zīxùn huò bié de zīxùn, suǒyǐ jíshǐ jiǎnxùn cúnzài zìjǐ de shǒujī lǐmiàn, yě bù huì yǒu gè zī xièlòu. Jíshǐ yì diào rényuán qù zhǎo tā huò fēnxiǎng yīxiē tǒngjìshùzì, diànjiā yě zhǐ huì zhīdào zújì shíjiān, juéduì bù huì yǒu gè zī. Lìngwài, diànxìn shāng zài shōu dào jiǎnxùn 28 tiān hòu yīdìng huì shānchú, zhè shì jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì zhǐyǐn de yāoqiú. Zhīqián google biǎodān háishì cúnzài, wǒmen méiyǒu yào tìdài jì yǒu de rènhé shí lián zhì fāng'àn, wǒmen tígōng lìng yīzhǒng shí lián zhì xuǎnzé, xīwàng ràng dàjiā fāngbiàn, zhè wánquán gěi diànjiā juédìng. ## Diànjiā zài ménkǒu xūyào quèrèn shénme dòngzuò? Quèrèn gùkè sǎo mǎ de jiǎnxùn yǒu dài chū diànjiā dàimǎ, bìngqiě gùkè yǒu sòngchū shí lián zhì jiǎnxùn dào 1922. ## Shìfǒu qiángzhì?(2021.05.19) Mùqián wéi gǔlì jí tuīguǎng, bǎohù zìjǐ bǎohù biérén, dàn ruò wèilái yìqíng qiángdù shēngjí, xū qiángzhì shǐyòng shí, wǒmen jiù yǐjīng shàngshǒule.(Cǐ huídá shíjiān wèi 2021/05/19 11:30) # ==Mínzhòng/gùkè Q&A== ## LINE nèi jiàn sǎomiáo qì méi fǎnyìng zěnme bàn? Mùqián LINE nèi jiàn sǎomiáo qì bù zhīyuán zhège jīzhì, tuījiàn shǐyòng `jí guǎnjiā'de sǎomiáo gōngnéng. Qǐng jiārù `jí guǎnjiā'chéngwéi LINE hǎoyǒu, diǎn xuǎn `shí lián zhì sǎo mǎ', huò shǐyòng shǒujī shàng de xiàngjī jìn háng sǎomiáo, yì kě lìngwài xiàzài QR Code sǎomiáo qì. ## Fāsòng jiǎnxùn huì yǒu fèiyòng ma? Jiǎnxùn fèiyòng yóu diànxìn shāng quán'é fùdān, wánquán miǎnfèi, qǐng ānxīn shǐyòng. ## Tián wán zhège hái xūyào tián qítā de biǎodān ma? `Jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì'xìtǒng shì shí lián zhì qízhōng yīzhǒng fāngshì, yǔ zhǐ běn dēngjì jí gè diànjiā zìxíng zhìzuò diànzǐ biǎodān bù chōngtú, zé yī tiánxiě jí kě. Cǐ xìtǒng kuàisù jiǎnbiàn, yě kě quèbǎo zīxùn zhèngquè xìng, bìng lìyú yì diào zhuīzōng guǐjī, huānyíng dàjiā duōduō shǐyòng. ## Wǒ de gè zī huì cún dào nǎ? Zhèyàng ānquán ma? `Jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì'xìtǒng zūnzhào [`COVID-19 fángyì xīn shēnghuó yùndòng: Shí lián zhì cuòshī zhǐyǐn'](https://Www.Cdc.Gov.Tw/Category/Page/vNnZ-o-dP6JmsXEIf2Ix8Q), yī zuìshǎo qīnhài yuánzé qǔdé zuìshǎo de gèrén zīliào. Mínzhòng sòngchū de jiǎnxùn jǐn bāohán `chǎngsuǒ dàimǎ',`shǒujī hàomǎ'hé `shǒujī hàomǎ jìnrùchǎngsuǒ de shíjiān', yǐbiàn rìhòu tōngzhī quèzhěn zhě, quèzhěn zhě qùguò de chǎngsuǒ hàn kěnéng jiēchù zhě. Jiǎnxùn nèiróng jiāng cúnfàng zài gāi shǒujī suǒshǔ de diànxìn shāng, xiàn fángyì mùdì shǐyòng, bǎoliú 28 tiān jí huì shānchú. ## Tóngxíng zhǐyào yīrén tiánxiě jiù hǎo, háishì měi gèrén yào gèbié tián? Jiànyì měi gè yǒu shǒujī de rén dōu gèbié sǎo mǎ, rúguǒ quèdìng wǎng hòu kě liánluò dào tóngxíng de rén, yóu yīrén dàibiǎo yì kě. ## Wǒ de xiǎohái méiyǒu shǒujī, wǒ yě bùxiǎng yòngzhǐ běn shí lián fāngshì, kěyǐ zěnme zuò? Kěyǐ zhíjiē huífù yǐ sòngchū de jiǎnxùn, lìrú `+2', jí biǎoshì yǒu liǎng wèi tóngxíng zhě, bìng liúcún zài jiǎnxùn jìlù zhōng, zuòwéi yì diào cānkǎo. ## Diànxìn shāng bù huì jiāng jiǎnxùn jìlù zuò mùdì wài lìyòng ma? Yīfǎ bùxíng. Zhèxiē nèiróng jūn zūnxún shí lián zhì zhǐyǐn, jǐn gōng yì diào shǐyòng, yú 28 tiān hòu shānchú jìlù. Lìngwài, diànxìn yèzhě bù huì shōu dào chǎngsuǒ dàimǎ hé diànjiā de duìzhào biǎo, bù huì zhīdào chǎngsuǒ dàimǎ fēnbié dàibiǎo nǎxiē diànjiā, tígōng yì diào rényuán de jǐn yǒu chǎngsuǒ dàimǎ hé shíjiān qū kuài. ## Rúguǒ wǒ de shǒujī sǎo mǎ gōngnéng gùzhàng zěnme bàn? Qǐng fāsòng jiǎnxùn dào 1922, shǒudòng shūrù chǎngsuǒ dàimǎ, sòngchū jí kě. ## Rúguǒ wǒ de mén hào bùnéng fāsòng jiǎnxùn? Wǒ wàngjì dài shǒujī? Wǒ shēnshang dài de zhuāngzhì méiyǒu xíngdòng wǎng lù gōngnéng? Qǐng pèihé diàn jiā de zhǐ běn shí lián zhì, liú xià liánluò fāngshì. ## Rúguǒ sòngchū jiǎnxùn cái hòuhuǐ bùxiǎng jìnqù shāngdiàn, gāi zěnme bàn? Kě zhíjiē huífù yǐ sòngchū de jiǎnxùn, lìrú `wèi rù chǎng', yǐ liúcún zài jiǎnxùn jìlù zhōng, zuòwéi yì diào cānkǎo. ## Qǐngwèn táiběi tōng App yǔ jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì, chúle qūyù yǐwài, qítā de chābié shì? Duìyú yǒu ānzhuāng táiběi tōng APP mínzhòng, wǔ yuèdǐ qián kě jīngyóu APP de sǎomiáo qì zhí sǎo `jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì'QRcode, jí kě jìlù yì tiáo zīliào. Ruò wú ānzhuāng, zé kěyóu jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì QRCODE qǐdòng jiǎnxùn gōngnéng, fāsòng 1922 jìlù yì tiáo zīliào. Èr zhě jūn yóu zhèngfǔ jiànlì de fángyì shí lián zhì xìtǒng, duì gè zī bù luòdì yú shāngjiā duān, fēngcún gōng yì diào xūqiú 28 tiān hòu zìdòng xiāochú, mínzhòng kěyǐ gèng ānxīn jí biànlì zhīyuánzé shì yīzhì de. Zhǔyào chāyì diǎn jiǎn liè rúxià: 1. Jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì xìtǒng wúxū ānzhuāng APP, táiběi tōng bìxū xiān ānzhuāng jí zhùcè. 2. Táiběi tōng yǐ APP jì shù wéi zhǔ, hòuzhě zé yǐ jiǎnxùn xìtǒng wéi zhǔ, yīn'ér èr zhě zài cāozuò tǐyàn huì bùtóng. 3. Jiǎnxùn shí lián zhì QR-Code zhīyuán mínzhòng zhǔ sǎo, ér táiběi tōng APP kě zhī yuán mínzhòng zhǔ sǎo jí bèi sǎo de shí lián zhì chǎng yù. ## Gāngcái shuō dàzhòng yùnshū gōngjù jiǎnxùn shí lián zhǐshì yùbèi, méiyǒu qiángzhì, nà wǒ de yìsi shì shuōmíngtiān wǒ yào qù dā gāotiě, wǒ yào zuò ma? # ==Store & Place Manager Q&A==

## I have already implemented a real-link system, do I still need to use this system?

The focus is on providing a "real-link system." The "SMS real-link system" system is one of the methods of the real-link system, and it does not conflict with paper-and-pencil registration and the self-made forms of each store. The government will not force the use of this system.

This system is convenient for tracking the trajectory of the epidemic, which can ensure the accuracy of the information while taking into account the efficiency. It is recommended that you use it more.

## Do I need money to apply for this?

This system is completely free, please feel free to use it.

## How do I apply for a QRcode for the store?

Applications are open at 3 pm on 5/19.

1. Enter the "[SMS site QR Code registration operation](https://emask.taiwan.gov.tw/real/)" system.
2. Provide the serial number or ID number, mobile phone number, and after confirmation, you can get a QRcode containing the location code.

The location code is randomly generated and does not contain business registration or location information.

Any type of agency, unit, or individual business person can apply for a new QRcode at any time.

## How do you create branches? Is it the editor of the head office? Or the shop number?
The head office can generate the QR Code of each branch, or authorize each branch to add the branch number (only numbers) to the head office's store code and then manually generate the QR Code.

## I have not used the "mask real-name system" pre-order system before, how do I obtain the QRcode?
You can log in through multiple methods such as "Mobile Authentication", or "Health Insurance Autotoll", "TW FidO", "Health Insurance Card", "Natural Person Certificate" and so on.

## If people fill in their phone number, how can the store confirm it?
Instead of filling in the phone number, people use their mobile phones to scan the QRcode to send a text message to 1922, and show the completed screen of the text message to the staff of the place. 1922 will record the mobile phone number of the text message.

## I am a mobile vendor, where should I post the QRcode? What should I do if the guests will not bring their mobile phones?
This system is one of the methods of the actual linkage system, and it does not conflict with paper and pen registration and the self-made forms of each store. If there are things that are difficult to adopt, other methods can still be selected.

## What should I do if my QR Code is stolen and posted in another location?

If you find such a situation, please notify 1922.

## If the public is recommended to install the "Taiwan Social Distance" app, can it replace the text message?

Welcome to recommend the installation of the "Taiwan Social Distance" App, but stores that need to implement the actual linkage system still need to do so through any means including the "SMS actual linkage system" system.

(According to the guidelines for the actual linkage system, the Taiwanese social distancing app is currently unable to provide the necessary contact information for the epidemic.)


## Nowadays, some company numbers or residential communities require real-name registration when people come, so what identity do they choose? (0519 Press Conference Yang Guang asks questions)

The main difference between a legal person and an individual store is whether there is a unified organization. If there is, the legal person store is used. If not, like an apartment building, as long as there is a representative, you can use the personal store to apply.

## In this app, does the SMS outgoing area have settings for how long to keep the data and how long to delete it? Now there are many stores that need to use the google form designed by the Ministry of Health and Welfare. Will this form be used in the future? Still consider calling for the use of the current app. (0519 press conference TVBS question)

This is not an app, so there is no question of how long the mobile phone will last. The outgoing text messages will stay in the mobile phone, and whether it is deleted or not depends on everyone.

The only record that the SMS program will leave is the location code. There will be no store address, latitude and longitude information or other information, so even if the SMS is stored in your mobile phone, there will be no data leakage.

Even if the epidemic investigators go to him or share some statistics, the store will only know the footprint time and will never have any information. In addition, the telecommunications company will delete the newsletter 28 days after receiving it. This is a requirement of the guidelines for the implementation of the newsletter system.

Previously, google forms still existed. We did not want to replace any existing implementation schemes. We provide another implementation option, hoping to make it convenient for everyone. This is entirely up to the store.

## What action does the store need to confirm at the door?
Make sure that the customer scan code has brought out the store code, and the customer has sent the real-link SMS to 1922.

## Is it mandatory? (2021.05.19)
At present, to encourage and promote, protect ourselves and protect others, but if the intensity of the epidemic escalates in the future, when the use of it is mandatory, we have already started. (This answer time is 2021/05/19 11:30)

# ==People/Customers Q&A==

## LINE What should I do if the built-in scanner does not respond?

Currently, the built-in scanner of LINE does not support this mechanism. It is recommended to use the scanning function of "Disease Butler".

Please join "Disease Butler" to become a LINE friend, click "Real-Link Scan Code", or use the camera on your phone to scan, or you can also download a QR Code scanner.

## Will there be a fee for sending the newsletter?

The cost of the newsletter is fully borne by the telecom company and is completely free. Please feel free to use it.

## Do I need to fill in other forms after this?

The "SMS real-link system" system is one of the methods of the real-link system. It does not conflict with the paper registration and the electronic forms made by each store. You can fill in one of the options.

This system is fast and simple, can also ensure the accuracy of the information, and is conducive to tracking the trajectory of the epidemic. You are welcome to use it more.

## Where will my personal information be deposited? Is this safe?

The "Newsletter System" system complies with the ["COVID-19 New Life Campaign for Epidemic Prevention: Guidelines for Implementation System Measures"] (https://www.cdc.gov.tw/Category/Page/vNnZ-o-dP6JmsXEIf2Ix8Q), according to The principle of least infringement obtains the least personal data.

The text messages sent by the public only contained the "location code", "mobile phone number" and "time when the mobile phone number entered the premises" so as to inform the confirmed person, the places where the confirmed person has been, and those who may be contacted in the future.

The content of the short message will be stored in the telecom company to which the mobile phone belongs, and used for epidemic prevention purposes only. It will be deleted after being kept for 28 days.

## As long as one person fills it out in the same group, or should each person fill it out individually?

It is recommended that everyone who has a mobile phone scan the code individually. If it is determined that the person in the same group can be contacted in the future, it is also possible to have one representative.

## My child does not have a mobile phone, and I don’t want to use the physical connection method. What can I do?

You can directly reply to the sent text message, such as "+2", which means that there are two companions and keep it in the text message record as a reference for the epidemic situation.

## Will the telecommunications company not use the newsletter records for other purposes?

Not according to law. These contents follow the guidelines of the actual linkage system and are only used for epidemic investigation, and the records will be deleted after 28 days.

In addition, the telecom operators will not receive the comparison table of the location code and the store, and will not know which stores the location code represents, and only the location code and time block are provided for the epidemic investigation personnel.

## What should I do if the scan code function of my mobile phone fails?

Please send the newsletter to 1922, manually enter the location code and send it.

## What if my door number cannot send SMS? I forgot to bring my mobile phone? The device I’m wearing doesn’t have a mobile network function?

Please cooperate with the store’s paper-based system and leave contact information.

## What should I do if I send out the newsletter and regret not wanting to enter the store?

You can directly reply to the sent newsletter, such as "not admitted", and keep it in the newsletter record as a reference for the epidemic situation.

## Except for the area, what is the difference between the Taipei Tong App and the SMS system?

For those who have installed the Taipei Tong APP, they can directly scan the QRcode of the "SMS" through the APP scanner before the end of May to record the epidemic information. If there is no installation, the SMS function can be activated by QRCODE, and 1922 recorded epidemic information can be sent.

Both of the two are established by the government to establish a real-link system for epidemic prevention. The personal information does not fall on the merchant side, and the need for sealing and supplying the epidemic will be automatically eliminated after 28 days. The principle that the public can be more comfortable and convenient is the same.

The main differences are summarized as follows:
1. There is no need to install an APP for the SMS real-time system. Taipei Connect must be installed and registered first.
2. Taipei Tong is mainly based on APP technology, while the latter is based on SMS system, so the operating experience of the two will be different.
3. The SMS-based QR-Code supports the public's main scan, and the Taipei Tong APP can support the public's main scan and the real-linked field that has been scanned.

At present, it is planned to put it together at the intersection of the station and the place where the body temperature is measured.
