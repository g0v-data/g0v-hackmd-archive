Newer cryptos such as Cardano use a fraction of the energy of Bitcoin and Ethereum due to a mining alternative called “proof of stake.” If we were to do this project on Ethereum, the enegy cost to create the NFT and send it out would likely cancel out any benefit the donation to a partner organization would provide. With Cardano there is hardly any energy expended to create an NFT. Thus, when we sell an NFT and send part of the proceeds to a carbon sequestering organization, the NFT itself becomes in a way “carbon negative,” in other words a Carbon Sink!
