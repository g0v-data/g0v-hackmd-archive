#### **Market Segmentation**

The Construction Elastomers Market can be segmented by type, chemistry, application, and region:

**By Type:**

1\. **Thermoset Elastomers:** Known for their heat resistance and durability, thermoset elastomers are widely utilized in construction applications that require high performance and long-term stability.

2\. **Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE):** These materials combine the properties of rubber and plastic, making them easy to process and recycle. Their versatility is a key factor driving their adoption in the construction sector.

7\. **Butyl Rubber (IIR)**

8\. **Acrylic Copolymer (ACM) Elastomer**

9\. **Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV)**

10\. **Silicone (Q) Elastomer**

11\. **Others**

**By Application:**

1\. **Roofing:** Elastomers provide excellent weather resistance and durability, making them ideal for roofing applications.

2\. **Flooring:** The properties of elastomers contribute to flexibility and resistance to wear and tear in flooring solutions.

3\. **Sealants and Adhesives:** Used for bonding and sealing, construction elastomers enhance the performance of sealants and adhesives.

4\. **Insulation:** Their insulating properties make them suitable for various thermal and sound insulation applications.
