> Guideline:  What can I do when I feel helpless?
> 
######  This BOT is not a tool, it is a guideline for  user to reach out themselves for help.
###### 

#### So basically I had the idea to creat a chatbot where people could access to all the resources. In the meantime talk through things going on.
####
