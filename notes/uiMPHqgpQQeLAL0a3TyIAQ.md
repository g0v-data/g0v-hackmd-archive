## How to join

:::success
We will use [g0v Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/user/g0vTW) for livestream.
:::
### 💪 報名、寫下三個關鍵字自我介紹 Register
Graphical Instruction：http://bit.ly/hackath38nrigester

以往大家最容易詞窮的「三個關鍵字自我介紹」，將是本次活動報名的頁面。使用 @ronnywang 開發的網站，搭配 g0v Slack 帳號，讓大家自行輸入自己的暱稱、關於自己的三個關鍵字，可以自己錄音，也可以讓 Google 小姐幫你念，輸入完成等同報名完成，將會在自我介紹時段輪播大家的錄音歡迎大家前往報名，一起成為線上百人黑客松的一份子。

🔔 請大家一定要進行報名，才能夠完整參加整個黑客松，在分組開工時，進入視訊會議室！

The "3 keywords self introduction" is required by this time's registration page.  We are using the webpage developed by @ronnywang.  You will login with g0v slack account, key in 3 keywords about you, and (optionally) record 3 keywords with your own voice (otherwise, We will use Google translate to read your 3 keywords).  Everyone's 3 keywords will be played during the "Self Introduction" section.  Everyone is welcome to join this online hackathon.

🔔 Please do remember to register, so you can have a complete experience.  And please join the VC room of your project while hacking.

### 💪 三分鐘提案、短講、成果發表 3-min proposal
List：http://bit.ly/g0v38thpropose `Deadline: 09 MAR, 12:00 GMT+8`

無論是想要在 g0v 提案、發表短講，或是為自己的專案招兵買馬，歡迎在列表上填入相關資訊，讓參與者都能夠線上看見你的專案。坑主請一定要留下 g0v Slack ID，方便揪松團幫你增加視訊會議室的坑主權限～

Feel free to add yourself to the list, you can
1. Propose a new project
2. Give a (short) talk
3. Recruit for your project

Add yourself to the list so people can find your project. The owners of projects must provide their g0v Slack ID, so people can reach you, and organizer can grant VC room permission to you.


### 💪 分組 Hacking
Channels for each project： `Coming Soon`

在提案和自我介紹結束後，參與者可以選擇自己有興趣的專案，加入視訊會議一起討論，本次主要使用的視訊會議軟體為 meet.jit.si，黑客松客製化視訊介面開發中，請關注活動共筆，獲得最新資訊。

After the Self Introduction and 3-min Proposal section, participants can join the project they are interested in, and join the VC room to discuss with project owner.  This time we will use meet.jit.si for VC room.  We are still working on our own version, please keep an eye on the share doc.

### 💪 活動照片 Photos

每次的黑客松，都會有攝影師幫大家記錄下活動過程，本次在家黑客松，g0v 揪松團提出一個 Hashtag 照片牆的構想，鼓勵參與者將當天吃的午餐、點心，或是在家開工的照片，上傳至臉書活動頁面，或是個人的 SNS 平台，並加上「`#g0vhackfromhome`」的標籤。

Please take photo when you join g0v Hackath38n at home, and use hashtag `#g0vhackfromhome` when you post it on FB or SNS.
