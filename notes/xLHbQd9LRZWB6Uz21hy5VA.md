# Transifex 介面使用教學

> [點此觀看原始內容](https://g0v.hackpad.tw/jyE1bqw4QX4)


transifex是一套翻譯協作平台

### 註冊

連到 [https://www.transifex.com/signup/](https://www.transifex.com/signup/)
輸入帳戶名稱、email、密碼
**啟用**帳號

### 加入團隊

連到 [https://www.transifex.com/projects/p/ccp-topics/](https://www.transifex.com/projects/p/ccp-topics/)
點選 **Chinese**  **(Taiwan) **
![](https://g0vhackmd.blob.core.windows.net/g0v-hackmd-images/upload_d86edec9a1ed7a4bbf1dac52517ff40c)
點加入團隊
![](https://g0vhackmd.blob.core.windows.net/g0v-hackmd-images/upload_c987b38760c31439fceeb99a7b5033c2)

勾選**我已閱讀**並同意該專案的[貢獻者許可協定](https://www.transifex.com/projects/p/ccp-topics/language/zh_TW/)。
點**馬上加入**

### 開始翻譯

選擇右上角的翻譯(**Translate**)
![](https://g0vhackmd.blob.core.windows.net/g0v-hackmd-images/upload_77faf31be0884cf624072ec8d89c71c4)
點選**語言**
![](https://g0vhackmd.blob.core.windows.net/g0v-hackmd-images/upload_bd1830262f1e23606d4df50481d1c974)
點選**資源**
![](https://g0vhackmd.blob.core.windows.net/g0v-hackmd-images/upload_dfb27621f849d8ed3abe1d06a43ceb69)
選擇字串
填入翻譯
點選**保存**
![](https://g0vhackmd.blob.core.windows.net/g0v-hackmd-images/upload_1f6b7dad602a15a2176e3c93ba802fb8)
