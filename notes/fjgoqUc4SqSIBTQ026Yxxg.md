Ford V8

The Ford V8 is a highly iconic engine series produced by Ford Motor Company, known for its powerful performance and durability. First introduced in 1932 with the Ford Flathead V8, it became the first affordable V8 engine for mass production. Over the years, Ford has developed several V8 engines, including the Windsor, Cleveland, and modular V8s, widely used in cars, trucks, and racing vehicles. Ford V8 engines are celebrated for their torque, horsepower, and versatility, making them a popular choice for enthusiasts, muscle cars, and heavy-duty applications.
