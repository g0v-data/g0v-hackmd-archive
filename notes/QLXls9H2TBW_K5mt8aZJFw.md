Greetings from the g0v Summit 2024 Program Committee,

First and foremost, we would like to extend our gratitude for your submission to the g0v Summit 2024. As the deadline for submissions is fast approaching, with less than a week left, we sincerely invite you to seize this opportunity to update your submission. This will ensure that your presentation aligns with the latest progress of your project, enabling you to share the most current insights with the community.

Additionally, if you know of friends or colleagues who are also contributors to civic technology and open-source culture, and who might be interested in sharing their achievements and experiences, we encourage you to share the submission link with them as well.

Should you have any questions or require further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us via email.
