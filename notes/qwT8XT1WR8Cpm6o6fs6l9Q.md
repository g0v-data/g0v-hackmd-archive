# Common Mistakes Drivers Make with Car Oil Changes in Dubai

![Story pin image](https://i.pinimg.com/736x/8c/64/65/8c6465522108ff3e03212f565b5b4d2c.jpg)

Dubai's climate, traffic patterns, and long driving distances place extra stress on vehicle engines. While a car oil change may seem like a simple routine task, many drivers unknowingly make mistakes that reduce engine life, affect performance, and increase repair costs. In a city where high temperatures are the norm, engine oil plays a critical role in cooling, lubrication, and overall engine protection.

Service My Car regularly assists Dubai drivers who face engine issues caused by improper oil change practices. Understanding the most common mistakes drivers make with car oil changes in Dubai can help vehicle owners avoid unnecessary damage and keep their cars running smoothly for years.

Delaying Oil Changes for Too Long
---------------------------------

![](https://albertthomas.bcz.com/c/u/zT/rj/gUc_7q/SWjI6RUT8Y9Ble4eel0JAV735RQ6-3tbeqtEzvBybSE/image.jpeg)

### Why This Is a Problem in Dubai

●      Extreme heat breaks down oil faster

●      Stop-and-go traffic increases engine strain

●      Old oil loses its lubricating properties

Delaying oil changes causes increased friction, overheating, and accelerated engine wear. In severe cases, it can lead to engine failure.

Using the Wrong Type of Engine Oil
----------------------------------

![](https://albertthomas.bcz.com/c/u/zT/rj/gUc_7q/RKqSDsd0sXUK6Nf56__phpr6ufDB2I9pzXmZdlSVHeI/image.jpeg)

Not all engine oils are the same. Using oil that does not meet manufacturer specifications is a serious mistake.

### Common Oil Selection Errors

●      Using mineral oil instead of synthetic

●      Choosing incorrect oil viscosity

●      Ignoring manufacturer recommendations

In Dubai's high temperatures, incorrect oil types thin out faster and fail to protect engine components properly. Always use oil suitable for both the vehicle and the climate.

Ignoring Oil Quality and Brand Standards
----------------------------------------

![](https://albertthomas.bcz.com/c/u/zT/rj/gUc_7q/F_AOgKhL7zU8_gcr8N_yTEZP901Ps2v4tiKqy1qSmjQ/image.png)

Some drivers choose low-quality oil to save money, unaware of the long-term damage it can cause.

### Risks of Poor-Quality Engine Oil

●      Faster oil breakdown

●      Increased engine deposits

●      Reduced engine efficiency

Low-quality oil may look acceptable initially but fails under prolonged heat and pressure. This leads to sludge buildup and costly repairs over time.

Forgetting to Replace the Oil Filter
------------------------------------

![](https://albertthomas.bcz.com/c/u/zT/rj/gUc_7q/vzWKzm2YwbTYNusg5CYTfIfjGduji6owi6XbkcEaVFw/image.png)

Changing engine oil without replacing the oil filter is another common mistake.

### Why the Oil Filter Matters

●      Traps dirt and metal particles

●      Keeps oil clean

●      Protects engine components

A clogged oil filter contaminates fresh oil almost immediately, reducing its effectiveness and shortening engine life.

Overfilling or Underfilling Engine Oil
--------------------------------------

![](https://albertthomas.bcz.com/c/u/zT/rj/gUc_7q/HzcycK5X0HS9VdWk4g3LGt7x4QxA31sg3q6RN9zAhxc/image.png)

Maintaining the correct oil level is crucial. Too much or too little oil can both cause serious issues.

### Problems Caused by Incorrect Oil Levels

●      Overfilled oil can damage seals and gaskets

●      Underfilled oil causes poor lubrication

●      Increased engine heat and friction

Professional oil change services ensure the oil level is always accurate.

Ignoring Oil Leaks
------------------

![](https://albertthomas.bcz.com/c/u/zT/rj/gUc_7q/oIErPLlGZPV4G6qcF8DlEfgEBVOQJxvM716hFAx9ulg/image.png)

Small oil leaks are often ignored by drivers, especially when there are no immediate symptoms.

### Why Oil Leaks Should Never Be Ignored

●      Gradual oil loss leads to engine damage

●      Oil leaks can damage surrounding components

●      Risk of engine overheating

Regular inspection during oil changes helps detect leaks early and prevents major mechanical failures.

Relying Solely on Dashboard Alerts
----------------------------------

![](https://albertthomas.bcz.com/c/u/zT/rj/gUc_7q/bEoWRG4TlPGE-LByv4ciwtZNXUMq32EARjJykA4yVEU/image.png)

Modern vehicles come equipped with oil warning lights, but relying solely on these alerts is a mistake.

### Why Warning Lights Are Not Enough

●      Alerts often appear after damage has begun

●      Sensors may not detect oil quality

●      System delays can occur

Manual inspection and scheduled servicing are far more reliable for engine protection.

Skipping Oil Changes for Low-Mileage Driving
--------------------------------------------

![](https://albertthomas.bcz.com/c/u/zT/rj/gUc_7q/0-Bm8AOUcD1zrWV2UWFw_K5qowE89sGZ8A0oJcec2pw/image.png)

Many drivers assume that if they don't drive much, oil changes are unnecessary.

### The Truth About Low-Mileage Driving

●      Oil degrades over time, not just mileage

●      Short trips cause moisture buildup

●      Engine doesn't reach optimal temperature

Even low-mileage vehicles require regular oil changes, especially in hot climates like Dubai.

Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

### How often should I change my car oil in Dubai?

Most vehicles require oil changes every 5,000 to 10,000 kilometers, but Dubai's heat may require shorter intervals.

### Is synthetic oil better for Dubai weather?

Yes, synthetic oil performs better in high temperatures and provides superior engine protection.

### Can skipping an oil change damage my engine?

Yes, delayed oil changes increase friction, overheating, and can lead to severe engine damage.

### Does low mileage mean I can delay oil changes?

No, oil degrades over time even if the car is not driven frequently.

### Should I replace the oil filter every time?

Yes, replacing the oil filter with every oil change ensures clean oil circulation and engine protection.
