CS2 has its own way of handling skins and items that is worth understanding before spending anything on the market. Cases are the main source of new skins entering the game but you can also get basic weapon drops just from playing regularly. The market side is where things get more interesting — prices shift based on supply, condition, and how popular a particular finish is at any given time. Float value and wear condition are the two things that catch most newer players off guard because two listings of the same skin can look similar but carry very different prices. I went through [learn more](https://dmarket.com/cs2-wiki/) when I was getting started and it covers all of this in a straightforward way without overcomplicating things. Useful reference before making any market purchases
