> 1. How to scale the methodology to online volunteers
>
> Our system allows volunteer editors to put counter-narratives to messages collected from closed messaging chatrooms. We do not put any verification measures to our editors to maximize the collaboration, but we have not found a way to successfully educate the editors on how to write successful replies that will circulate in messaging app context. We are really interested in learning the guidelines of creating text narratives that is impactful. I learned a lot today, it will be even better if there is any material on the topic already exists and can be shared with us.

>
> I am one of the speaker at the microtargeting landmark session tomorrow (Friday, June 14):

> - 2:15pm @ Oya 3 (Laico) : Voter Beware! Lessons from the ground https://sched.co/QF3o
