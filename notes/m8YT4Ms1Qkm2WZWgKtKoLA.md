- 1 time unit = 1tu = 30 mins
- 1 proposal = 1p
- 1 talk = 1p, 1tu (30 mins)
- 1 panel = 3p, 3tu (90 mins)
- 1 workshop = 1p, 2tu (60 mins)
- 1 day 1 place = 9tu (上午 3tu 下午 6tu）
