## What is QSR Digital Signage?
 QSR digital signage involves making use of dynamic digital screens or displays like menu boards, drive-thru displays, and in-lobby screens to display content. This is much more than mere replacement of printed menus. It includes the tactical presentation of colorful food images, dynamic menu changes, promotional videos, and even edited social feeds. This is aimed at establishing a diverse, interactive and very efficient communication medium with the customers.
## Important Advantages to Your Fast Food Restaurant
 The benefits of implementing a digital signage solution can be measured:
Upselling and higher average transaction value: Energetic cartoons and combo specials subtly influence customer decision-making, which is a successful way of marketing high-profit goods.
Operational agility: Modify menu items, prices or promotional activities in all locations in real-time on a central platform. There is no need to reprint or keep outdated signage.
Improved brand experience: HD images and uniform communications reinforce brand recognition and provide a technologically advanced, modern experience to the customer.
Better accuracy of orders: Dynamic and transparent point of sale and drive-through displays help customers make confident decisions, which reduce errors and accelerate service.
Dayparting made easy: Automatically alternate between breakfast menus, lunch menus and dinner menus with preset timings and provide ideal relevance all through the day.
