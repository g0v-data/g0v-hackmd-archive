**Impact of Russia-Ukraine War on Nano Fertilizer Market**

In 2023, fertilizer prices soared to unprecedented levels, exacerbated by Russia's invasion of Ukraine in February. This further strained a global supply chain already weakened by worker shortages and factory closures induced by the COVID-19 pandemic since 2020. These initial disruptions marked the onset of instability in the fertilizer market, with global prices rising by 30% by early 2022, following an 80% surge in 2021, as reported by the World Bank. Consequently, the war significantly affected the nano fertilizers market.
