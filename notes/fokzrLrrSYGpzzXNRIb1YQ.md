# 2022 11th World Movement for Democracy (WMD) Global Assembly in Taipei

## 前情提要 Background info
- Time: 2022/10/24-27 4 days
- Location: Taipei
- [官網 Official site](https://www.movedemocracy.org)
- [這次大會的新聞稿 Press release](https://www.movedemocracy.org/announcing-the-11th-global-assembly-of-the-world-movement-for-democracy)
- 主辦單位為 WMD，美國 NED 為其秘書處
    - Host is WMD, NED (of the US) is its secretariat
- chihao 服務的單位 [IORG](https://iorg.tw/about) 獲邀擔任這次大會的委員會成員之一 
    - chihao’s day job [IORG](https://iorg.tw/about) has been invited to be one of the committee member of this assembly
- 委員會也有其他 3 個組織，分別是[勵馨基金會](https://www.goh.org.tw/)、[台灣同志諮詢熱線協會](https://hotline.org.tw/)、[臺灣青年民主協會](https://www.taiwanyad.org/)
    - 3 other committee members are [Garden of Hope](https://www.goh.org.tw/), [Taiwan Tongzhi (LGBTQ+) Hotline Association](https://hotline.org.tw/), and [Taiwan Youth Association for Democracy](https://www.taiwanyad.org/)
- 大會委員會成員職責之一是邀請公民社會成員與會
    - One of the duties of a committee member is to invite civil society members to participate in this assembly
- chihao 希望邀請 g0ver & FtO organizers 與會
    - chihao would like to invite g0vers and FtO organizers to participate
- 大會與會者機票應由 WMD 補助，住宿由台灣民主基金會補助
    - Plain tickets to Taipei would be (partially) sponsored by WMD, and accommodations in Taipei is sponspored by Taiwan Foundation for Democracy (TFD)
