Modern iPhones use laminated displays. Apple's product documentation confirms that glass, touch layer, and display panel work as a single unit. That is why most professional iphone screen repair processes replace the complete display assembly. This method restores colour accuracy, brightness balance, and touch sensitivity.

If your screen looks like modern art after a drop, it is not a new feature. It is a repair signal.
