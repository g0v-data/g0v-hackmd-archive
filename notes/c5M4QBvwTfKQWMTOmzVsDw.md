# How to find g0vers at [NPO Hub Taipei](https://www.npohub.taipei/) 

## Who is where?
g0v-jothon has a office in NPO Hub Taipei.

Address: No. 2, Section 3, Chongqing South Road, Zhongzheng District, Taipei City, 100

g0v-jothon is a g0v community task force.

## How to get in?

1. The street entrance looks like this. Press button on the door to enter.
![](https://g0vhackmd.blob.core.windows.net/g0v-hackmd-images/upload_b6b834685daac1b00de75de47b0598a4 =60%x)

2. Press doorbell next to the 2nd door. Door will be opened from upstairs.
![](https://g0vhackmd.blob.core.windows.net/g0v-hackmd-images/upload_f9fa0053fa66da9b4aa62cf8bcb2a506 =60%x)

3. Take the elevator to 4F.

4. Exit the elevator. Meeting rooms are to your right, the kitchen is to your left.
