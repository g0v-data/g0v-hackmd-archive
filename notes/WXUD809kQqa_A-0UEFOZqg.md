### **Market Overview**

Maleic anhydride is a versatile chemical compound primarily used in the production of unsaturated polyester resins (UPR), copolymers, and various other chemical intermediates. It is synthesized from n-butane or benzene, making it an essential ingredient in the manufacture of a range of products, from paints and coatings to plastics and adhesives. The growing trend towards sustainable and eco-friendly materials further drives the demand for maleic anhydride, given its suitability in the production of bioplastics and biodegradable polymers.

### **Market Segmentation**

The Maleic Anhydride market can be segmented by raw material, application, end-user industries, and regions, providing a comprehensive overview of its landscape.

#### **By Raw Material**

l **N-Butane:**\
N-butane is one of the primary raw materials used in the production of maleic anhydride. Its availability and cost-effectiveness have made it the preferred choice for many manufacturers. The conversion of n-butane to maleic anhydride is a well-established process, leading to consistent supply and quality.

l **Benzene:**\
Benzene is another crucial raw material used in producing maleic anhydride. The utilization of benzene allows for the synthesis of high-purity maleic anhydride, catering to applications requiring stringent quality specifications. However, the volatility of benzene prices can impact production costs.

#### **By Application**

l **Unsaturated Polyester Resin (UPR):**\
UPR is the largest application segment for maleic anhydride, accounting for a significant share of the market. Its widespread use in the automotive, marine, and construction industries drives demand, as UPR offers excellent strength, durability, and resistance to corrosion.

l **Copolymers:**\
Maleic anhydride-based copolymers are used in various applications, including adhesives and sealants. The demand for high-performance adhesives in construction and automotive sectors is propelling the growth of this segment.

l **Lubricant Additives:**\
The inclusion of maleic anhydride in lubricant formulations enhances their performance, promoting its use in automotive and industrial applications. This segment is expected to witness steady growth due to the increasing demand for efficient lubricants.

l **Alkenyl Succinic Anhydrides:**\
Alkenyl succinic anhydrides derived from maleic anhydride find applications in drilling fluids and oilfield chemicals. The growth in the oil and gas sector contributes to the increasing demand for these specialized chemical additives.

l **Malic Acid and Fumaric Acid:**\
Maleic anhydride is a precursor to malic and fumaric acids, which are widely used in the food and beverage industry. The rising demand for natural and organic food products drives the growth of this segment.

l **Others:**\
Other applications of maleic anhydride include its use in the production of surfactants, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products, further diversifying its market scope.
