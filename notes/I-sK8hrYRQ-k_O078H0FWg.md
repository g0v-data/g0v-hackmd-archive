* 資料:https://data.london.gov.uk/dataset?q=&tag=planningconstraintsmap
* 格式:geopackage檔案
* 資料細節：
    * 需要避免的圖資：
        * Designated Open Space

        * Ancient Woodland
        * Strategic Industrial Locations (SILs)
        * Locally Significant Industrial Sites (LSIS) 
        
    * 需要考量的圖資：
        * Publicly owned land (based on public land registry https://apps.london.gov.uk/public-land/) 
        * Community asset e.g. church
        * Underused sites
