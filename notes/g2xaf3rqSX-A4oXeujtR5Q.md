- Nerd Politics vs. 數位治理：eID / 個人資料保護 / AI 科技規範 / 數位平台規管 / 數位性暴力 / 數位主權

g0v Summit 2024 Call for Proposals

g0v is a grassroots social movement community dedicated to deepening the civic engagements and connections between the social arena and citizens. You can find like-minded partners in the g0v community, put your ideas into practice in a grassroots manner, and then release the results under an open-source license, letting people stand on your shoulders.

g0v replaces the “o” in gov with “0”, thereby reimagining the role of government from scratch, and represents the vision from 0 to 1 of the generation of digital natives.

g0v Summit 2024 is scheduled from May 4th (Saturday) to May 5th (Sunday), 2024. The Summit will feature five main themes: “Nerd politics v.s. digital governance,” “Data, AI, and community collaboration,” “Grassroots, open, polycentric, and communal responses to political and technological authoritarianism,” “Digital infrastructure grounded in empathy, inclusiveness, and plurality,” and “Convergence of technology and social issues.” All themes will be accompanied by corresponding keywords aimed at exploring topics of open government, open-source collaboration with citizen participation, and/or sharing practical experiences in action.

The agenda for this Summit will be divided into three types of sessions: 30-minute presentations (suitable for sharing individual projects or topics), 90-minute thematic forums (suitable for inviting people from diverse backgrounds to engage in discussions), and 60 to 90-minute workshops designed for participants to engage in hands-on activities on-site.
We sincerely invite you to submit proposals and join us again. For more information, please see below.
