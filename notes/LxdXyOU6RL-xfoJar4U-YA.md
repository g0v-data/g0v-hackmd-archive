**Market Segmentation**\
The Liquid Ring Vacuum Pumps market can be segmented based on type, material, rate, application, and region:

**By Type**

1\. **Single-stage:** These pumps are known for their simplicity and efficiency in handling various applications, making them a popular choice in several industries.

2\. **Two-stage:** Providing higher vacuum levels, two-stage liquid ring vacuum pumps are ideal for more demanding applications, contributing significantly to market growth.

**By Material Type**

1\. **Cast Iron:** Valued for its durability and cost-effectiveness, cast iron remains a common choice in various applications.

2\. **Stainless Steel:** Known for its corrosion resistance and hygiene properties, stainless steel is increasingly preferred in the pharmaceutical and food sectors.

3\. **Others:** This category includes materials such as plastics and composites that are tailored for specific applications.

**By Rate**

1\. **25 -- 600 M³/H**

4\. **Over 12,000 M³/H**\

**By Application**

1\. **Petrochemical & Chemical:** A significant market share is attributed to the petrochemical and chemical industries, where liquid ring vacuum pumps are used for various processes, including distillation and evaporation.

2\. **Pharmaceutical:** The need for stringent quality and hygiene standards in pharmaceutical production drives the adoption of these pumps.

3\. **Food Manufacturing:** Ensuring the safety and quality of food products is paramount, further increasing the use of liquid ring vacuum pumps in this sector.

4\. **Others:** Additional applications include aircraft, automobile manufacturing, water treatment, oil and gas extraction, power generation, expanded polystyrene (EPS) and plastics production, pulp and paper manufacturing, among others.
