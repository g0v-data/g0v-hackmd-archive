Universal Industrial Services
![](https://g0v.hackmd.io/_uploads/ryJbnyhBkg.jpg)
Universal Industrial Services are experts in architectural, structural and sheet metalwork, balustrade, staircases, and other fabrication tasks in a multitude of specifications, including mild steel, stainless steel, aluminium and other metals. The company covers all aspects of design, detail, machining, manufacture, fabrication and installation. Our services include bespoke fabrication, plant access, mezzanine floors, bespoke staircases, glass balustrade, steel handrails, balconies, steel beams, steel lintels, gates, crane operations, and welding services. We are accredited to BS EN 1090 Execution class 2 and have, Alcamus safe contractor and CHAS advanced membership.
